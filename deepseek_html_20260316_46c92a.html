About Us

At Tandoori Treat Café, we bring the heart of Punjab to your plate. Every dish is crafted in our traditional tandoor using time‑honored recipes. From fluffy Butter Naan to sizzling Paneer Tikka, creamy Dal Makhni, and our signature Soy Chaap — we use only the freshest ingredients and authentic spices. Come taste the warmth of North India.
